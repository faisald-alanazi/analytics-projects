# AI Video Marketing Research & Consumer Analytics

## Project Overview

Conducted a comprehensive marketing research study analyzing consumer perceptions, adoption drivers, usage behaviors, and purchasing intentions related to AI-powered video creation platforms using InVideo as the primary case study.

The project combined exploratory research, customer interviews, survey development, statistical analysis, and business recommendations to identify opportunities for improving customer acquisition, retention, and product adoption.

---

## Business Problem

AI video generation platforms face several business challenges:

* High customer acquisition costs
* Low free-to-paid conversion rates
* Customer retention issues
* Increasing competition within the generative AI market
* Limited product differentiation

The objective was to identify customer needs, barriers to adoption, willingness to pay, and feature preferences to support future product and marketing strategy decisions.

---

## Research Objectives

* Understand user perceptions of AI-generated video content
* Evaluate adoption drivers and usage patterns
* Measure willingness to pay for AI video platforms
* Identify ethical and trust-related concerns
* Compare perceived effectiveness across business, educational, and personal use cases
* Develop actionable business recommendations

---

## Research Methodology

### Exploratory Research

* Industry analysis
* Competitor analysis
* Customer review analysis
* Secondary market research

### Qualitative Research

Conducted in-depth interviews with:

* Students
* Marketing professionals
* Business managers
* Healthcare professionals
* Technology researchers

Interview themes included:

* AI tool familiarity
* Video creation behavior
* Adoption motivations
* Ethical concerns
* Product expectations

### Quantitative Research

Designed and distributed a structured survey containing:

* 26 research questions
* Multiple hypothesis tests
* Likert-scale measurements
* Demographic segmentation

Survey Results:

* 71 survey responses collected
* 67 completed responses used for analysis

---

## Key Findings

### Adoption Drivers

Top motivations for using AI video tools:

* Time savings
* Ease of use
* Reduced technical requirements
* Cost efficiency

### Content Preferences

Most commonly created content:

* Educational videos
* Social media content
* Business and marketing videos

### User Concerns

Major concerns identified:

* Misinformation
* Deepfakes
* Data privacy
* Job displacement

### Product Improvement Opportunities

Users expressed strong interest in:

* More realistic AI voiceovers
* Better editing capabilities
* Free templates
* Multilingual functionality

---

## Business Recommendations

### Product Strategy

* Improve voice realism
* Expand editing flexibility
* Increase personalization options

### Marketing Strategy

* Focus messaging on educators, marketers, and content creators
* Highlight productivity gains and ease of use
* Reduce onboarding friction

### Trust & Governance

* Clearly label AI-generated content
* Improve transparency around data usage
* Address ethical concerns proactively
